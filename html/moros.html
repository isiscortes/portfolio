Moros con Cristianos

A shortfilm comedy. A distracted nun gets dressed to attend mass. When she leaves her apartment and walks downstairs she remembers she left her stove turned on, and decides to go back and turn it off. However, when she arrives she realizes the door is open, scared, she enters the room to see who is the intruder. Once inside there are things that don't belong there. Bewildered, she wonders around the living room when she realizes there is a semi-naked man in the room cooking. She gets stubborn arguing that it is her property until she realizes that she was wrong.

Directed by: Izaak Martinez

First Assitant Director (1AD) & Video Editor: Isis Cortes

Director of Photography: Stefano Murillo

Sound Design / Sound Editor: Pablo Rueda
