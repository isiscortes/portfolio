Totem VR

A virtual tour of the sculpture Totem, by Aldo Chaparro. In his work, totems are a tools that seek to connect the earthly with what is above, with the divine. They represent man´s desire to reach freedom, for this reason you can hear whispers in different languages saying “freedom” because freedom is a universal theme.
