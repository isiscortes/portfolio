Justo a Tiempo

Justo a Tiempo by Isis Cortes Sauza on Scribd

Just in Time was a student project that focused on generating a TV series Pilot episode. Six young fellas are surrounded in drama, they share the fact that there is no time left before their countdown begins, which will affect their destiny, when the watch marks zero their lives will change forever. Diverse tragedies interconnect stories that these young fellas are unaware of, showing that one decision can alter the life of a person for better or for worse.
