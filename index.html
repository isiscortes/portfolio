About


Isis Cortes immersed herself in the world of animation and programming while studying her Bachelor's Degree of Animation and Digital Art at ITESM CEM (Monterrey Higher Institute of Technology). High fashion and an international lifestyle (living in Mexico City, London, Amsterdam, Boston, Budapest, Barcelona, Madrid, Milan, and Rio de Janeiro) shaped her creative eye to become vibrant, dynamic, and conceptual.

Clients include UPSOCL, FastMag (Formula1), Interjet, America Movil, World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF), Museo Soumaya and Los Espiritus de Gante. Her fine art has been sold and presented at different galleries around the world including the Museum of Fine Arts Boston, the Institute of Contemporary Art Boston, Isabella Gardner's Museum and Casa Lamm.

She currently resides in York, United Kingdom.
